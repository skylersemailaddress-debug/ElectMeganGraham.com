Why Megan

Because this office should run better.

The Elkhart County Clerk’s Office is not a symbolic role. It is an operating office that manages elections, records, filings, and public-facing systems that matter every day.

The problem

When procedures are unclear or systems break down, the public feels it through confusion, delays, weak communication, and reduced trust.

The standard

Residents deserve an office that is organized, accountable, transparent, and easier to use.

The answer

Megan’s campaign is built around better systems, stronger service, and practical, visible improvements.

Campaign Contrast

This is a competence campaign.

The focus is not on drama. It is on making the office more reliable, more transparent, and more citizen-centered from the inside out.

What that means in practice

Better service starts with better systems.

  • Clearer citizen communication
  • Stronger election information and transparency
  • More reliable records and workflow handling
  • Visible standards for service and accountability

Short version

Megan is running because the Clerk’s Office should work better for the people who depend on it.
