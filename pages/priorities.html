Priorities

Clear priorities for a more reliable Clerk’s Office.

This campaign is organized around three operating priorities that tie directly to the work of the office and the daily experience of the public.

Secure Elections

Strengthen transparency, communication, and public-facing trust around election administration so voters feel confident in the process.

Reliable Records

Improve organization, procedures, and accessibility around the records residents and professionals rely on.

Professional Service

Run the office with clearer systems, better service standards, and a more citizen-first approach to daily operations.

Citizens First Service Model

Easy Government. Professional Systems. Transparent Elections.

These priorities reflect how the office should serve residents day to day: with clearer communication, stronger systems, and visible accountability.

  • Easy Government: clearer paths, simpler interactions, less friction
  • Professional Systems: consistent procedures, reliable workflows, accountable operations
  • Transparent Elections: visible trust-building in how the office communicates and serves voters
