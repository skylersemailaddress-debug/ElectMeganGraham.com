Meet Megan

A campaign built on professional systems, public trust, and practical service.

Megan Graham is running for Elkhart County Clerk because this office should work better for the people who rely on it. The campaign is focused on organized systems, reliable service, transparent elections, and a citizen-first approach to county government.

Who She Is

Grounded, practical, and focused on how government actually serves people.

This campaign is not built around noise. It is built around competence. Megan’s message is that county government should be easier to use, more accountable, and more professional in the way it handles public service.

  • Citizen-first mindset
  • Operational focus instead of empty slogans
  • Clear standards for service, records, and election administration

Core campaign promise

Bring stronger systems, clearer procedures, and more reliable public service back to the Clerk’s Office.

Why Megan See Priorities

Community Focus

The Clerk’s Office touches daily life for families, businesses, attorneys, employers, and voters. That requires service that is dependable and respectful of people’s time.

Professional Standards

This campaign is centered on stronger workflows, visible accountability, and office systems people can trust.

Public Trust

Trust is built through transparency, consistency, and an office that does what it says it will do.
