Why Systems Matter

Government works best when it runs on clear systems that serve the public.

Marriage licenses, court filings, public records, and election administration all depend on an office that is organized, accountable, and ready to serve residents well.

Meet Megan

A campaign grounded in professionalism and public service.

Megan Graham is running for Elkhart County Clerk because the office should be organized, trustworthy, and easier for residents to use. Her message is simple: clear systems build public trust.

First 100 Days

A practical roadmap, not vague promises.

This campaign is built on a professional roadmap for election transparency, stronger internal controls, better records workflows, and a better citizen experience.

  • Review office workflows, bottlenecks, and service breakdowns
  • Improve election information, transparency, and citizen-facing communication
  • Strengthen records organization, procedures, and accountability
  • Launch a Citizens First feedback process and visible service standards
